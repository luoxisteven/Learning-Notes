# 可数名词和不可数名词
- 测试
  - https://agendaweb.org/grammar/countable_uncountable-exercises.html

## 可数名词
- data
  ✅ There are more data coming.

## 不可数名词
1. **抽象名词**
  - advice
    - a piece of advice, some advice
  - information
    - a piece of information, some information
  - progress
    - make progress, some progress
  - behavior
    - human behavior, unusual behavior
  - evidence
    - a piece of evidence, strong evidence
  - knowledge
    - a bit of knowledge, vast knowledge
  - research
    - a piece of research, conduct research
  - homework
    - do homework, a set of homework
  - work
    - do work, a piece of work
  - courage
    - show courage, a lot of courage
  - education
    - receive education, higher education
  - luggage / baggage
    - a piece of luggage, check baggage
  - furniture
    - a piece of furniture, some furniture
    - ❌ many informations / furnitures
    - ✅ much information / furniture

2. **食物类（整体物质）**
  - rice
    - a bowl of rice, a grain of rice
  - bread
    - a slice of bread, a loaf of bread
  - meat
    - a piece of meat, some meat
  - fruit
    - a piece of fruit, some fruit
  - cheese
    - a piece of cheese, a block of cheese
  - water
    - a glass of water, some water
  - coffee / tea
    - a cup of coffee, a cup of tea
    - if counting: a piece of / a cup of / slices of …

3. **学科**
  - math / mathematics
    - study math, a course in mathematics
  - physics
    - study physics, a branch of physics
  - chemistry
    - study chemistry, a field of chemistry

4. **材料**
  - water
    - a glass of water, a bottle of water
  - oil
    - a drop of oil, some oil
  - air
    - a breath of air, fresh air
  - wood
    - a piece of wood, a block of wood
  - paper
    - a piece of paper, sheets of paper
    - 注意：paper 表“报纸”时可数 (a paper / newspapers)
  - cloth
    - a piece of cloth, some cloth
  - plastic
    - a piece of plastic, some plastic

5. **天气现象**
  - rain
    - heavy rain, a drop of rain, some rain
  - snow
    - heavy snow, a flake of snow, some snow
  - fog
    - thick fog, dense fog, a patch of fog
  - sunshine
    - bright sunshine, warm sunshine
  - news
    - a piece of news, breaking news, good/bad news

6. **语言与信息类不可数**
  - content
    - video content, high-quality content, some content
  - equipment
    - a piece of equipment, some equipment, technical equipment
  - software
    - install software, software update, software tools
  - data
    - large amounts of data, data analysis, data collection
      - （注意：现代英语中 data 可数不可数皆可，但学术场合常视为复数）

7. **其他常见不可数**
  - traffic
    - heavy traffic, rush-hour traffic
  - pollution
    - air pollution, water pollution, reduce pollution
  - money
    - a sum of money, some money, a large amount of money
  - time
    - spend time, a lot of time, spare time
  - space
    - free space, limited space, physical space
  - trouble
    - have trouble (doing sth), get into trouble, cause trouble
  - permission
    - ask for permission, give permission, obtain permission


## 既是可数名词，也可以是不可数名词
### 1. experience
- **不可数**：经验  
  - He has a lot of experience.
- **可数**：经历（一次事件）  
  - He had many interesting experiences during the trip.
### 2. work
- **不可数**：工作  
  - There is too much work to do.
- **可数**：作品  
  - The museum displays several works of art.
### 3. paper
- **不可数**：纸  
  - I need some paper.
- **可数**：论文、报纸  
  - I wrote two papers this semester.
### 4. business
- **不可数**：商业活动  
  - Business is slowing down.
- **可数**：公司  
  - Many small businesses are growing quickly.
### 5. time
- **不可数**：时间  
  - We don’t have much time.
- **可数**：次数  
  - I’ve been there three times.
### 6. light
- **不可数**：光  
  - There isn’t enough light in this room.
- **可数**：灯  
  - Please turn off the lights.
### 7. coffee / tea / beer / water
- **不可数**：液体  
  - She drinks too much coffee.
- **可数**：一杯、一份  
  - We ordered two coffees.
### 8. chicken / fish
- **不可数**：食物  
  - I ate some chicken.
- **可数**：动物  
  - There are three chickens in the yard.
### 9. glass
- **不可数**：玻璃（材料）  
  - The table is made of glass.
- **可数**：玻璃杯  
  - She bought two glasses.
### 10. hair
- **不可数**：头发整体  
  - She has beautiful hair.
- **可数**：一根头发  
  - I found a hair in my soup.
### 11. behavior / behaviour
- **不可数**：整体行为  
  - Human behavior is complex.
- **可数**：具体行为类型  
  - Several aggressive behaviors were observed.
### 12. change
- **不可数**：改变的概念  
  - Change is necessary for growth.
- **可数**：具体改变、多项变化  
  - Many changes happened last year.
### 13. process
- **不可数**：过程概念  
  - Learning is a continuous process.
- **可数**：具体步骤 / 程序  
  - This system includes three processes.
### 14. difficulty
- **不可数**：困难的整体概念  
  - He had difficulty sleeping.
- **可数**：具体难点  
  - The test contains several difficulties.
